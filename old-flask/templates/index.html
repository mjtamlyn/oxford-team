The Oxford Team Tournament

An exciting new shoot coming February 2013

The Oxford Team Tournament is a new archery competition designed for teams. Three archers shoot a FITA 18m qualification round, followed by team knock out matches, using Beiter Hit-Miss targets.

Let me know when entries open

The competition is open to teams of three archers, either all recurve or all compound. The ranking round is a normal FITA 18, shot on 3 spot faces. The matches which follow are shot on Beiter Hit-Miss targets. Each match consists of 24 arrows per team shot over 4 ends of 2 minutes each, highest number of hits wins. These targets are a great spectacle, and huge fun to shoot at. For an example of what they're like, check out the final of the British Universities Team Championships 2012 on YouTube.
